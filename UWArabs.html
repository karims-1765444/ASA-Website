Mission Statement

The Arab Student Association at the University of Washington aims to unite and serve the Arab community on campus. The ASA shall act as a bridge between the Arab community and non-Arab community by educating the general populace about Arab culture and heritage. We are committed to acceptance and peace as we aim to serve both Arabs and non-Arabs through our events and mission to enrich our campus with diversity.
